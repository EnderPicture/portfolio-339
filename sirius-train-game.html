Sirius Train Game

What is this

A "calming" game where you get to drive three different types of trains, the bullet train, diesel train, and steam train. You have to walk around in the engineer's cabin tweaking all the knobs and dials to get the train to start, cruse, and park on time and comfortably.

Created for SFU's Fall 2019 class of IAT 410 - Advanced Game Design.

What did I do

I am the lead programmer and scene creator in the team creating all the main game logic including the infinitely generating terrain system, player controller, train controller, scoring system, and train physics.

Responsible for vector art such as all three of the trains and menus.

Worked with artists and sound designers closely to integrate them into the game.

Completed game within 1 month.

Awards

With a panel of industry judges, our game won the "Certificate of Excellence in Game Design" and "Winning the Best Game (Champion)" award.
